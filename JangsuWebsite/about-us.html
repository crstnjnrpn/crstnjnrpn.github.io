About Us

Welcome To Jangsu Korean Store,

Your Trusted Neighborhood Source For Authentic Korean Products Here In Dasmarinas, Cavite. Whether You're Craving Familiar Favorites Or Exploring New Flavors, We Bring A Little Piece Of Korea Closer To Your Home.

OUR STORY

Jangsu Korean Store started as a small family-run shop with a simple goal: to provide the local community with quality Korean groceries, snacks, and household essentials. Over time, we've grown into a go-to destination for Korean food lovers, students, and families in Cavite and nearby areas.

Despite our growth, we noticed that many of our customers still had to visit the store just to check product availability, promotions, or store hours. This often led to long waits, miscommunication, and missed opportunities to serve you better.
